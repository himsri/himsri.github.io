World is very colorful but due to color blindness some can not differentiate between certain color.This condition results from an absence of color-sensitive pigment in the cone cells of the retina, the nerve layer at the back of the eye.For of this genetic and hereditary defect, over 400 million people worldwide are colorblind.
There are three cones of color and three type of color blindness.
Normal Eye Graph
Color Blind Eye Graph
  1. Blue - Tritan
  2. Green - Deuteran 
  3. Red - Protan 
Mixing of them creates the particular color.If one cone malfunction then a eye has a very difficult time in differentiating the exact color.But some of degree of detect can be overcome by using technology.
Enchorma glasses help people with red/green color blindness see the full spectrum of color.It help them differentiate between the red and green.These glasses are made in such way that when a photon into it changes the frequency upto a limit that a color blind eye can differentiate and see amazing colors.
